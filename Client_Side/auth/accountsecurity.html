Account Security Guide

Welcome to Shun Resort! Keeping your account safe is important to us. Follow these simple tips to protect your account and personal information.

1. Use a Strong Password

Create a unique password that includes a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using easily guessed words like your name or birthday.

2. Don’t Share Your Login Details

Never share your password or verification code with anyone. Shun Resort staff will never ask for your password via email or chat.

3. Watch Out for Suspicious Emails

If you receive an unexpected email asking for personal details or claiming to be from Shun Resort, do not click any links. Always check the sender’s address carefully.

4. Keep Your Device Secure

Make sure your computer or mobile device has updated antivirus software and avoid using public Wi-Fi when logging into your account.

5. Reset Your Password Regularly

Change your password every few months to keep your account secure. If you think someone else might have access, reset it immediately.

6. Contact Support If You Notice Anything Strange

If you suspect any unauthorized access or suspicious activity on your account, contact Shun Resort support right away for assistance.
